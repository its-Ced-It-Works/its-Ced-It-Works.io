DD Glance

Dimension Data Glace is a fourth place project that was demoed at Dimension Data's Hackfest 2019.1 held by its Building_20. It is a AV system checker that uses the scanning of QR codes to enable frictionless ticket logging for Dimension Data employees.


DD Dash

Dimension Data Dash is an employee enablment tool for Dimension Data Security. It acts as an encylopedia of the various security controls the Security division deals with. It's poised to answer three question related to the controls; What is it, Why's it important; What's the potential risk of not having it in place
